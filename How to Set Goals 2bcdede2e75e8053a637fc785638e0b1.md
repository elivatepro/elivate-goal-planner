### Why set goals

1. To give yourself a sense direction
2. You care about your vision
3. To create a clear path towards your vision

### Kinds of goals

1. Believe Goal (70% close to your reality)
    1. What you would achieve if everything goes right
    2. This is your MAXIMUM RESULT
    3. This will not entail your imaginations or superstitious thoughts
    4. It will not entail your IFs, your excuses
    5. **When should you send it:** Between the 15th of the current month and the 1st of the next month.
2. Affirmation Goal (100% reality)
    1. What you can achieve with the time, resources and effort you have currently at your disposal geared towards achieving your believe goal.
    2. What your efforts and contributions has earned you.
    3. What you will be reaping after your 30 days of sowing.
    4. The potential result of your believe goal.
    5. It is a report of your results.

### How should you set goals

1. Use the SMART technique of setting goals.
    1. Be Specific
    2. Your goal should be Measurable
    3. Your goal should be Achievable
    4. Your goal should be Realistic
    5. Your goal should be Time-bound
