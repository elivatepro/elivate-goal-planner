## Having the know-how of how to set my goals and how goals can push me towards attainment.

### How to set director goals

1. I have to have a motivation for setting the goal
    1. There is need for DESIRE
    2. Something needs to motivate me
    3. My motivation has to be STRONG.
    4. It must be attached to what I dim important in life.
- We learn to win by winning.
1. Things to consider when setting goals: **Identify the minimum, realistic and the dream goal.**
    - The minimum goal (Bare minimum)
    - The realistic goal
    - The dream goal
2. Identify my minimum, realistic and dream goals
    1. 750QPV
    2. 1000QPV
    3. 2000QPV
3. Believe goal we set in the business fall under the **Dream Goal.**
4. After setting goal the next thing is to work based on the Dream goal (Believe Goal)
    1. I work like I want to make my dream goal my reality
        1. Thoughts are things
        2. It is not over until it is over
        3. You cannot think two thoughts at the same time
        4. Dream works better when people don’t believe it
    2. Create an activity that ensure that dream goals become an activities
        1. I write the activity I need to do to make the dream goal come to pass
    3. Creating the activities means a game plan has been created
    4. Goal gives direction, activity is the game plan.
5. Convert that activity to a habit
    1. Habits are what guarantees the execution of your activities.
        1. Such that as long as those habits are in place, you find it easy to execute the game plan.
        2. Habits are lock and key that makes your plans executable.
        3. They should be concise and short.
        4. Habits are about setting up my environment to make executing my plans possible and mandatory.
            1. It makes me disciplined effectively.
    2. When you go after your dream goal, you will always do better than your minimum goal.
- When setting up the new believe goal, look at the activities that didn’t produce the result, and then change the activities and see if the activities need to be switched up.
